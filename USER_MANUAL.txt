HOUR RECTIFICATION STANDALONE SKILL — USER MANUAL
==================================================

What this file does
--------------------
This file gives Claude the instructions it needs to help you figure out a person's
unknown birth hour (Hour Pillar) in BaZi (Chinese astrology). When a client doesn't
know their birth time, Claude will narrow down all 12 possible Hour Pillars to a
shortlist of 1-3 likely candidates — using known life facts as evidence.

No installation required. Just attach this file to any Claude chat and use the
prompt below to get started.


How to use it
-------------
1. Download this .md file from GitHub
2. Open Claude (claude.ai or any Claude app — no paid plan required)
3. Start a new chat and attach this file
4. Send the following prompt:

   "Follow the skill instructions in this file and help me rectify the
   birth hour for [name], DOB [date of birth], [Male/Female]."

That's it. Claude will read the file and guide you through the full process.


When to use it
--------------
- A client's birth time is unknown or unconfirmed
- You want to figure out which hour best fits a person's known life story
- You want to complete a BaZi chart with an unknown 4th pillar


What you need before starting
------------------------------
1. The person's date of birth (year, month, day) and their sex
2. Known life facts about the person — see below for what's useful

No other skills or prior chart preparation required. Claude will calculate
and calibrate the 3-pillar chart before beginning the rectification process.


How the process works
----------------------
The skill works through a structured elimination process across 6 steps:

STEP 0 — 3-pillar calibration
Claude first calculates the Year, Month, and Day Pillars from the date of birth,
assesses the Day Master's strength, and identifies the useful/harmful elements.
This takes about a minute and sets up everything needed for rectification.

STEP 1 — Generate all 12 Hour Pillars
Claude calculates all 12 possible Hour Pillars for the person's Day Master
using the Five Rat Escaping formula. Each hour has a unique stem, branch,
and set of hidden stems.

STEP 2 — Build a candidate table
Claude maps out how each of the 12 hours interacts with the existing 3-pillar
chart (clashes, harmonies, combinations, punishments).

STEP 3 — Quick eliminations
Some hours can be ruled out immediately based on chart structure alone —
for example, if an hour would catastrophically destroy the person's Day Master
or double an already-overwhelming harmful element.

STEP 4 — Cross-reference known life facts
This is the core of the process. Claude tests each remaining candidate against
known facts about the person's life, including:

  - Siblings: how many, and their genders
  - Children: how many, and any pregnancy complications
  - Parents: death years, relationship with mother/father
  - Spouse: was the partner dominant and forceful, or stable and conventional?
  - Health: known illnesses and which part of the body they affect
  - Career: what field they work in
  - Personality: any striking traits not yet explained by the 3-pillar chart
  - Life arc: whether their best and worst decades match what the chart predicts
    (this is often the strongest filter before using the ZWDS app)

STEP 5 — Interactive questioning
Claude asks targeted questions in order of elimination power — starting with
questions that will rule out the most candidates at once (e.g. rough time of
day, sibling count). You don't need to know all the facts upfront; Claude will
guide you through what to ask the client.

STEP 6 — Shortlist and recommendation
Claude narrows the candidates down to 1-3 options, explains the reasoning for
each, and recommends running the remaining candidates through the Destiny Reading
Calculator app to compare ZWDS palace configurations as a final tiebreaker.


What the final output looks like
----------------------------------
At the end, Claude produces a clear summary such as:

  Remaining candidates: [丑时, 酉时]
  Most likely: 酉时 — because [reason A, reason B]
  Runner-up: 丑时 — because [reason C], but [concern D]
  Eliminated: [all other hours with brief reasons]
  Recommended next step: Run both hours in the Destiny Reading app and
  compare the 命宫, 夫妻宫, and 疾厄宫 palaces

The skill pairs with the Destiny Reading Calculator app (by the same author)
which generates full ZWDS charts for comparison.


Tips for best results
----------------------
- The more life facts you can provide, the more accurate the elimination
- Major life events with known years are especially useful (e.g. a parent
  dying in a specific year, or a major career shift)
- Public or historical figures with documented life timelines are ideal
  for learning the process, as facts can be verified independently
- If the client knows even a rough window ("definitely born at night" or
  "early morning"), this immediately eliminates half the candidates


Works best alongside

- Destiny Reading Calculator app (for ZWDS tiebreaker comparison)
